# Hook Phase Test Drive

This reference supports the hook automation rules SDLC.

Test drive puts the rule into realistic end-to-end scenarios.

1. Attempt at least five realistic scenarios.
2. Include at least one portability or alternate-runtime scenario.
3. Record PASS, PARTIAL, FAIL, or BLOCKED for each.
4. Capture the user-visible friction in one sentence.
5. Capture the follow-up change needed to remove that friction.
6. Use the test-drive skill to keep evidence structured.
7. Do not collapse partial outcomes into success.
8. Prefer real inputs over synthetic placeholders when safe.
9. Keep the friction report concise and actionable.
10. Test-drive evidence should feed the next iteration decision.
